My Skills.

Sit-Stay

Sit-Stay is a skill that I have mastered. It involves sitting and then staying until my owner tells me I can move again. This helps keep me safe and out of harm's way if something happens that could endanger me!

Leave It

Leave It is an important skill that I am highly proficient in. If asked to "Leave it" I will not interact with whatever I was previously interacting with or I will not interact with a new object my owner introduces like a tasty treat.
